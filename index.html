MiNAE: Mutation-independent Nucleosome Architecture Evaluation

2026

A cfDNA fragmentomics framework for hepatocellular carcinoma detection using fragment-coordinate data instead of tumor mutation sequencing.

Processed public FinaleDB data by computing fragment lengths, creating patient size-profile features, and validating models against cirrhosis and hepatitis B controls. Final held-out validation reached ROC-AUC 0.900 and PR-AUC 0.873.

PrISMA-RSC: Pancreatic Cancer Target Prioritization

2026

PrISMA-RSC stands for Pancreatic Immunotherapy Screening and Microenvironment Atlas with Robust Specificity-Aware Consensus.

Built a computational consensus algorithm to prioritize pancreatic cancer stromal immunotherapy targets by integrating tumor specificity, stromal relevance, robustness checks, and structure-aware target triage.

DNA Methylation Biomarkers for Lung Cancer Detection

Accepted

Co-authored an accepted IEEE-credited conference study using 7,240 genomic samples and methylation biomarkers including EGFR, PD-L1, SHOX2, RASSF1A, and PTGER4.

Engineered and validated a gradient-boosting model achieving AUC 0.983, 92.7% accuracy, and 98.2% recall.
